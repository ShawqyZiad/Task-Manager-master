Why use Task Master?

Task Master is a powerful tool to help connect owners, managers and supervisors with their employees to effectively assign tasks to them and include deadlines. In addition, employees can log into their portal to check their tasks for the given day and even look at future tasks for the week or further ahead, if applicable. Employees can mark the task as complete and may also upload a picture of the completed task.
